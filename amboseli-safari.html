About Amboseli Safari

Amboseli National Park is famous for its large elephant herds and stunning views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Enjoy game drives, bird watching, and cultural visits with the Maasai.

  • Highlights: Elephants, Kilimanjaro, Maasai culture
  • Best Time: June to October, January to February

Sample Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Arusha, transfer to Amboseli
  2. Day 2-3: Game drives and cultural visits
  3. Day 4: Return to Arusha or continue your adventure
