🐾 Wildebeest Migration – The Greatest Show on Earth

Witness one of the most spectacular wildlife events on the planet as millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles journey across the Serengeti and into Kenya’s Maasai Mara. Often called "The Greatest Show on Earth", this annual migration is a breathtaking display of nature, survival, and Africa’s raw beauty.

🌟 Highlights of the Migration

  • River Crossings: Watch vast herds brave crocodile-infested rivers, creating dramatic and unforgettable scenes.
  • Predator Encounters: Lions, leopards, hyenas, and cheetahs follow the herds, offering unique insights into Africa’s predator-prey dynamics.
  • Calving Season: January–March is calving season in the southern Serengeti, attracting predators and incredible photo opportunities.
  • Endless Plains: See seemingly infinite herds sweeping across the golden savannah, moving in rhythm with nature’s calendar.

🗓️ Best Time to Witness

January – March

Calving season in the southern Serengeti

June – July

Dramatic river crossings in northern Serengeti

October – November

Herds return south to the Serengeti
