Experience Tanzania’s Premier Northern Circuit Safari

Embark on an extraordinary journey through Tanzania’s renowned Northern Circuit—a region celebrated for its breathtaking scenery, iconic wildlife, and legendary national parks. With Lenga Safaris, discover the perfect blend of adventure, comfort, and authentic African wilderness.

🌍 Featured Destinations

Serengeti National Park

Witness the awe-inspiring Great Wildebeest Migration and explore endless savannahs teeming with lions, elephants, cheetahs, and more. The Serengeti offers unrivaled game drives and a true immersion into Africa’s wild heart.

Ngorongoro Crater

Explore the world’s largest unbroken caldera—a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a haven for the Big Five. The crater’s lush landscapes and dramatic vistas create a unique natural amphitheater for unforgettable wildlife encounters.

Lake Manyara National Park

Discover tree-climbing lions, vibrant flocks of flamingos, and diverse habitats ranging from forests to lakeshores. Lake Manyara’s compact beauty and rich biodiversity make every moment memorable.

Tarangire National Park

Enjoy exclusive safari experiences among ancient baobab trees and vast herds of elephants. The perennial Tarangire River attracts wildlife year-round, ensuring exceptional game viewing in a tranquil setting.

🗺️ Sample Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrive in Arusha – Welcome briefing and overnight stay
  • Day 2: Tarangire National Park – Game drives and sunset vistas
  • Day 3: Lake Manyara – Explore lakeside wildlife and birdlife
  • Days 4–5: Serengeti National Park – Full-day game drives and wildlife tracking
  • Day 6: Ngorongoro Crater – Descend for a final safari adventure
  • Day 7: Return to Arusha – Optional cultural tour and departure
