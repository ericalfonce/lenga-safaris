About the Northern Circuit

The Northern Circuit is Tanzania’s most celebrated safari route, featuring world-famous destinations like Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, and Tarangire National Park. Witness the Great Migration, spot the Big Five, and experience breathtaking landscapes.

  • Serengeti: Endless plains and the Great Migration
  • Ngorongoro: The world’s largest intact volcanic caldera
  • Tarangire: Majestic baobabs and huge elephant herds

Sample Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Arusha, transfer to Tarangire National Park
  2. Day 2: Game drive in Tarangire, overnight at lodge/camp
  3. Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater tour
  4. Day 4-6: Serengeti safari and migration tracking
  5. Day 7: Return to Arusha or Zanzibar extension
